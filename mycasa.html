What is myCASA?

myCASA is an online portal where you can:

  • Apply for an Aviation reference number (ARN)
  • Access AviationWorx, CASA's online learning and registration system
  • Submit a Remote Pilot Licence application
  • Submit a Flight Crew Pilot Licence application
  • View your licences, certificates and accrediations
  • View and apply for renewals of Remotely Piloted Aircraft Operator's Certificates (ReOCs)
  • Update your contact details

More online services will be available soon through myCASA.
