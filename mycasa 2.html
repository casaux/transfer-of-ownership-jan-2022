What is myCASA?

myCASA is an online portal where you can:

  • apply for an aviation reference number (ARN)
  • register drones (RPA) for yourself or for your organisation
  • get RPA operator accreditation
  • view your licences, certificates and accreditations
  • access AviationWorx, CASAâ€™s online learning system
  • update your contact details
  • manage roles and users for your organisation

Learn more about what services are available in myCASA.

We recommend using Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox or Microsoft Edge to access myCASA.
